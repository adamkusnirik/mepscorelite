Project Background & Motivation

After MEP Ranking finished its activity, there wasn't an objective tool for citizens to overview MEP activities. Members often cherry-picked activities they dominated or didn't make proper comparisons. This tool aims to make their activities more transparent and provide citizens with comprehensive, unbiased information.

Project Mission

Enhancing transparency through the publication of relevant information about MEP activities is one of the key prerequisites for better informing the public, enabling more qualified decision-making, and creating more effective pressure for improving decision-making processes, legislative development, and EU governance.

This project helps citizens, experts, and MEPs themselves gain insight into their own activity as well as the activity of their colleagues. By providing objective, data-driven analysis, we aim to strengthen democratic accountability and enable more informed citizen participation in European democracy.

Technical Information

Transparent Processing

The project maintains transparent data processing and calculation methods. The scoring algorithms are documented and verifiable.

Historical Coverage

Data covers the 8th (2014-2019), 9th (2019-2024), and 10th (2024-current) terms of the European Parliament, enabling historical comparisons.

Mobile-Responsive Design

The application is optimized for mobile devices and tablets, ensuring accessibility across all platforms with touch-friendly interface elements and responsive layouts.
